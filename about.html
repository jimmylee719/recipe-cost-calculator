我們的使命 (Our Mission)

「食譜成本計算機」的誕生，源於一個簡單的信念：無論您是專業餐廳主廚、烘焙坊老闆，還是充滿熱情的家庭廚師，您都應該擁有一個強大、直觀且免費的工具，來精準掌握每一份料理的成本。我們致力於消除繁瑣的計算過程，讓您能更專注於創造美味。

This Recipe Cost Calculator was born from a simple belief: whether you're a professional chef, a bakery owner, or a passionate home cook, you deserve a powerful, intuitive, and free tool to master the cost of every dish. We are dedicated to eliminating the tedious calculation process, allowing you to focus more on creating delicious food.
